This site aggregates resources for web development from around the web. Browse and bookmark :)

if you have cool links to contribute, add them via comments on the appropriate pages. Alternatively, contribute directly to the project as an honest-to-goodness open-source contributor.

Learning to code can be really hard.

It's not just you: don't give up!

Your first 100 hours are probably going to be especially difficult. Keep at it and it will get easier. Organize yourself and your projects, stay on top of where you are in your plan, reach out to others for help, support, and community, and don't lose heart.

Start with Paul Ford's brilliant "What is Code?" essay. Then come check out the full-stack roadmap!
